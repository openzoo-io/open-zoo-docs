# Verification process

In OpenZoo, only collections displaying the official OpenZoo verification badge will be showing in the Explore tab.

Having a collection verified 

Prior to being granted the verification badge by the OpenZoo team, creators must mint their NFT collections and once done, contact us via Telegram, Discord or email, at the following:

[Telegram verification link](https://t.me/openzoo_validation)
[Discord verification link](https://discord.gg/tT4v8H6UBH)
[Email verification](contact@openzoo.io)
